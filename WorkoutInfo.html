Workout Cheatsheet

Recommended goals: Running is the perfect exercise for losing weight and improving your endurance.
Average calories burned: Average of 350 calories burned per hour of running!
Tips for beginners: Improving your running form can help you run faster, more efficiently and comfortably, and with less stress on your body and reduced injury risk. Proper running form reduces your risk of fatigue and ensures that you are getting the most out of your run. Follow these tips to work on perfecting your form.
  1. Look Ahead
  2. Keep Hands at Your Waist
  3. Relax Your Hands
  4. Check Your Posture
  5. Relax Your Shoulders
  6. Keep Your Arms at Your Sides
  7. Rotate Your Arms From the Shoulder
  8. Don't Bounce

Recommended goals: Walking is the perfect exercise for losing weight and improving your endurance.
Average calories burned: Average of 285 calories burned per hour of walking!
Tips for beginners: Here are 7 walking tips for beginners that will make sure you get the most out of your walks.
  1. Wear good walking shoes
  2. Maintain good posture
  3. Use your arms
  4. Use a heel-to-toe stride
  5. Pay attention to your halfway point
  6. Be realistic about your current endurance level
  7. Increase your distance gradually
